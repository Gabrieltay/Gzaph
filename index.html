ProBowl Stats





Description

ProBowl Stats is a user-friendly application that helps to keep track of your bowling games at every practice or league games.

Ever played your best series but you don't remember when?
Do you remember your frames' score when you achieved your highest game?
How often did you get a strike or a spare for each game?

Compute detailed statistics of your games to monitor your progress and understand your strength/weakness.

Want to share your game play with your friends?
No problem, share your game scoresheet on popular social network platform. 2 ways of entering your game scores.
- Entry of every frame's pins fall
- Or just enter your score after each game

Description

Challenge yourself in this simple mathematics game.

How to play?
Do you remember your frames' score when you achieved your highest game?
In each puzzle, there are 4 random numbers. Use basic mathematical operations (+, -, x, /) on all 4 numbers to achieve a result of '24'. Each number can only be used once.
Solve as many puzzles within 5 minutes.
All the puzzles are solvable!

IPPT Calculator





Description

IPPT Calculator is an app that helps you to calculate your scores/points in this new IPPT scoring system.

With this app, you are able to
1) Determine the number of push-ups, sit-ups and 2.4Km timing to achieve the points
2) Determine the points achieved by the number of push-ups, sit-ups and 2.4Km timing.
3) Set your target award and set the respective goals for individual station.
4) Overview of scoring system based on your age.
